Founded in 1915, Cebu Eastern College (CEC) is the 4TH OLDEST EXISTING CHINESE SCHOOL in the Philippines. Its roots can be traced to Cebu Chinese School, and the subsequent merging with Cebu Sun Yat Sen High School which was founded in 1925; the school was born as the Cebu Chinese High School.
